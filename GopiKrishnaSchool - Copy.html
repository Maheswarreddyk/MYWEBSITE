Institute of Advanced Studies, India (IASI)

Academic Information

Programs and Courses Offered:

  • School Programs:
    • Primary School: Grades 1 to 5
    • Middle School: Grades 6 to 8
    • High School: Grades 9 to 12 with streams in Science, Commerce, and Humanities
  • College Programs:
    • Undergraduate Programs: B.Tech, B.Sc, B.Com, B.A
    • Postgraduate Programs: M.Tech, M.Sc, M.Com, M.A, MBA
    • Doctoral Programs: Ph.D. in various disciplines
    • Specializations: Engineering, Science, Commerce, Arts, Management

Accreditations and Affiliations:

  • Accredited by NAAC with an 'A+' grade
  • Affiliated with the University Grants Commission (UGC)
  • Recognized by the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) for school programs
  • International affiliations with universities in the USA, UK, and Australia

Admission Process

Eligibility Criteria:

  • School:
    • Primary School: Age 5+ at the time of admission
    • Middle School: Promotion from the previous grade
    • High School: Minimum 60% in Grade 8 for Grade 9 admission
  • College:
    • Undergraduate: 10+2 with minimum 60% marks
    • Postgraduate: Bachelor’s degree with minimum 55% marks
    • Doctoral: Master’s degree with minimum 60% marks

Entrance Exams:

  • School: Not applicable for primary and middle school; entrance test for high school
  • College:
    • Undergraduate: JEE, NEET
    • Postgraduate: CAT, GATE
    • Doctoral: University-specific entrance test followed by an interview

Application Procedure:

  • Online application through the official IASI website
  • Submission of required documents and application fee
  • Important dates for application opening, closing, and exam schedules

Selection Process:

  • School: Based on previous academic performance and entrance test for high school
  • College: Based on entrance exam scores and academic performance, personal interview for postgraduate and doctoral programs

Fee Structure

Tuition Fees:

  • School:
    • Primary School: ₹50,000 per annum
    • Middle School: ₹70,000 per annum
    • High School: ₹90,000 per annum
  • College:
    • Undergraduate: ₹1,50,000 per annum
    • Postgraduate: ₹2,00,000 per annum
    • Doctoral: ₹1,00,000 per annum

Additional Costs:

  • Lab fees, library fees, and other miscellaneous expenses (approx. ₹20,000 per annum for both school and college)

Scholarships and Financial Aid:

  • Merit-based scholarships covering up to 100% of tuition fees
  • Need-based financial aid available
  • Special scholarships for reserved category students and athletes

Campus Facilities

Infrastructure:

  • Extensive library with a vast collection of books and e-resources

Accommodation:

  • On-campus hostels with single, double, and triple occupancy rooms
  • Amenities include Wi-Fi, study rooms, common areas, and 24/7 security

Dining:

  • Multiple mess facilities offering diverse cuisines
  • Hygienic and nutritious food options

Healthcare:

  • On-campus medical center with qualified doctors and nurses
  • 24/7 emergency services and tie-ups with nearby hospitals
